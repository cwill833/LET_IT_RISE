Step EIGHT

  • Place your dutch oven (both pot and lid) into the oven and preheat the oven to 500ºF.
  • Dust one of your loaves in your baskets with rice flour.
  • Once the oven is preheated take out the dutch oven.
  • Flip the loaf into the pan of the dutch oven so that the floured side is down.
  • Using a serrated knife cut a square on the top of your loaf.
  • Place the lid of the dutch oven top and return both to the oven.
  • Bake your bread at 450ºF for 20 minutes.
  • After 20 minutes, remove the lid of the dutch oven and bake for 20 more minutes.
