Step SEVEN

  • Fold each corner of the dough onto itself so that the flour is on the outside of both the top and bottom of the dough.
  • Flip your dough over
  • Using your bench knife (or dough spatula) angle it at a 45° and slide it under your dough.
  • Keep repeating this from every angle until your dough is round and smooth on the surface.
  • Cover with a damp kitchen towel and let it rest for 20 minutes.
  • Lightly dust the top of your dough with flour.
  • Then flip your dough over so the floured side is down.
  • Using both hands pinch the dough edge that is closest to you, stretch it lightly, and then fold it ⅔ over itself.
  • Pinch the right and left side stretch them out like wings and fold them back onto your dough one at a time.
  • Using both hands pinch the dough edge that is farthest from you, stretch it lightly, and then fold it ⅔ over itself.
  • Grab the edge closest to you and roll your dough over.
  • Using your bench knife (or dough spatula) angle it at a 45° and slide it under your dough.
  • Keep repeating this from every angle until your dough is round and smooth on the surface.
  • Place a kitchen towel over a basket or bowl and flour the towel so your dough won’t stick to it.
  • Place the smooth side of your dough face down in the basket.
  • Cover the basket with another kitchen towel.
