  1. Picture 1/Stir
    • Use your hands to mix up your starter.
    • Use your dough spatula to scrape off the dough on your hands into the bowl.
    • Then clean off the sides of the bowl.
    • Put a kitchen towel over your bowl and place the bowl in a shaded place in your kitchen

Let the starter sit until tomorrow morning.

  1. Picture 2/Feed
    • Use your dough spatula to scoop out 80% of your starter into the trash
    • Add 25g of whole wheat flour, 25g white flour, and 50g of filtered water to your starter.
    • Mix it all together with your hands.
    • Use your dough spatula to scrape off the dough on your hands into the bowl.
    • Then clean off the sides of the bowl.
    • Put a kitchen towel over your bowl and place the bowl in a shaded place in your kitchen

Now we will let are starter sit until after dinner.

  1. Picture 3/Ready
    • Your starter is now ready! We can use it to make our leaven. If you do not have the time to bake wait until tomorrow morning and feed your starter again. Otherwise, let's get started.
